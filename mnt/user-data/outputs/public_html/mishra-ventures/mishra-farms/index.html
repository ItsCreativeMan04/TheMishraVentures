Our Story

We grow the food your family eats — but no one knew us.

Our family has farmed wheat and rice for generations in UP. Every season we'd load up our harvest and take it to the nearest gola bazar or ardhat — only to get paid whatever price the dalal decided.

We got tired of selling 40 quintals of wheat for ₹1,400/quintal when the same wheat sat on your grocery shelf at ₹35/kg. We decided to bridge that gap ourselves.

🎯 Our mission: Give every Indian family access to real farm food at honest prices — and give our farm a fair income in return.
